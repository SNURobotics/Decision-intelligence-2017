Task1 - Assemblying set-top box with HDMI and power cable.

Initial x,y positions of the settop box, HDMI and power cable can be chosen, 
they must be inside the given intervals otherwise nominal values will be selected. 

Distance between HDMI and power cable should be not too close. 

Initial Settings

settop x, y (x: -0.7 ~ -0.3, y: -0.6 ~ -0.1):
-0.5 -0.35
 
HDMI cable x, y (x: 0.2 ~ 0.3, y: -0.6 ~ -0.4):
0.25 -0.5

power cable x, y (x: 0.3 ~ 0.4, y: -0.6 ~ -0.4):
0.35 -0.5
